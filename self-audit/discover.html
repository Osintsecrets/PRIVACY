What is PII? Risk levels

PII = information that can identify, locate, impersonate you. Risk depends on context and uniqueness.

High risk

  • Phone numbers
  • Exact home/work address
  • Credentials, tokens, auth screenshots
  • Government IDs, DOB + full name + city combined

Medium risk

  • Emails (esp. primary), work place + role
  • Unique full names or rare usernames
  • Photos with location clues (street signs, plates)

Lower risk (often public)

  • First name alone
  • Generic interests without location

Even a name can be high risk if it’s very unique or linked with city, workplace, or relatives.
