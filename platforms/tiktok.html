TikTok — Privacy & Security

Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Path: Profile → ≡ → Settings and privacy → Account → Security

Web (browser)
  1. Go to tiktok.com and sign in.
  2. Click your profile picture (top right) → Settings and privacy.
  3. Follow this path: Profile → ≡ → Settings and privacy → Account → Security.
  4. Adjust the setting for Enable Two-Factor Authentication to your preferred option.
  5. Confirm: Save or close if a prompt appears.
Android app
  1. Open TikTok and tap your profile (bottom right).
  2. Tap the ≡ (top right) → Settings and privacy.
  3. Tap through: Profile → ≡ → Settings and privacy → Account → Security.
  4. Update Enable Two-Factor Authentication to the privacy level you want.
  5. Confirm: Leave the screen or accept any prompt to store the change.
iOS app
  1. Open TikTok and tap your profile (bottom right).
  2. Tap the ≡ (top right) → Settings and privacy.
  3. Tap through: Profile → ≡ → Settings and privacy → Account → Security.
  4. Update Enable Two-Factor Authentication to the privacy level you want.
  5. Confirm: Leave the screen or accept any prompt to store the change.
