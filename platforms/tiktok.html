TikTok

Practical privacy actions you can take today on TikTok.

No actions matched your search. Try different keywords or remove filters.

Navigation Primer

Mobile (TikTok app)

  • Profile tab: Bottom-right icon. Tap it to access your account controls.
  • Menu (☰): Top-right on your profile → opens Settings and privacy.
  • Settings search: Use the search bar within Settings and privacy to locate features quickly.
  • Accounts vs. Privacy: Account covers login details, Privacy handles visibility and interactions.

Desktop (tiktok.com)

  • Visit tiktok.com and sign in.
  • Click your profile picture (top-right) → Settings.
  • Use the left navigation for Security, Privacy, and Data controls.
  • Some features remain mobile-only; the desktop page links to the mobile app when needed.

If menus look different, tap the profile menu and use the Settings search tool for keywords like “2-step verification”.

Guides #1–12

Change Your TikTok Password

On Mobile
  1. Open TikTok and go to your profile.
  2. Tap the Menu (☰) → Settings and privacy → Account.
  3. Choose Password, verify via email or SMS, then set a new strong password.
On Desktop
  1. Go to tiktok.com → click your profile picture → Settings.
  2. Select Account → Password.
  3. Complete verification and enter a new password, then click Confirm.

Tips: Avoid reusing passwords; consider a password manager to store unique credentials.

Enable TikTok Two-Step Verification

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Security.
  2. Tap 2-step verification.
  3. Select Authenticator app or SMS (plus Email for backup) and follow the prompts.
On Desktop
  1. tiktok.com → Profile picture → Settings → Security.
  2. Click 2-step verification and enable your preferred methods.

Tips: Authenticator apps or security keys (if available) provide stronger protection than SMS.

Review TikTok Active Devices

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Security.
  2. Tap Manage devices.
  3. Remove any device you do not recognize by tapping the trash/bin icon.
On Desktop
  1. tiktok.com → Settings → Security.
  2. Select Manage devices, then Remove suspicious sessions.

Tips: Clear out old sessions after using public or borrowed devices.

Update TikTok Recovery Details

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Account → Account information.
  2. Tap Email and Phone number to add or update verified contact options.
  3. Confirm codes sent to your email or phone.
On Desktop
  1. In Settings, choose Account → Account information.
  2. Edit your email and phone, verify them, then save.

Tips: Keep recovery info private; avoid using work emails shared with others.

Make Your TikTok Account Private

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Privacy.
  2. Toggle Private account on.
On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy.
  2. Enable Private account and confirm.

Tips: Private accounts require you to approve every follower before they can see your content.

Limit TikTok Comments

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Privacy → Comments.
  2. Set Who can comment on your videos to Friends or No one.
  3. Turn on Filter keywords and add words you want to block.
On Desktop
  1. In Settings, open Privacy → Comments.
  2. Adjust who can comment and enable keyword filters.

Tips: Combine comment limits with keyword filters to minimize harassment.

Control TikTok Direct Messages

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Privacy.
  2. Tap Direct messages.
  3. Choose Friends or No one for who can send you DMs.
On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy → Direct messages.
  2. Select the strictest option available.

Tips: Disabling DMs prevents phishing attempts from strangers.

Limit TikTok Stitch and Duet

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Privacy.
  2. Select Stitch and set to Friends or Only me.
  3. Return and select Duet with the same restrictions.
On Desktop
  1. In Settings, choose Privacy → Stitch and Duet.
  2. Limit both features to trusted audiences.

Tips: Restricting Stitch and Duet prevents others from reusing your videos without approval.

Turn Off TikTok Profile Views

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Privacy → Profile views.
  2. Toggle Profile view history off.
On Desktop
  1. If available, go to Settings → Privacy → Profile views.
  2. Turn off the profile view history toggle.

Tips: Turning this off also hides who viewed your profile, keeping your browsing private.

Manage TikTok Ad Personalization

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Privacy → Ads.
  2. Tap Ad personalization and toggle off Use of off-TikTok data and other available switches.
On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy → Ads.
  2. Disable Ad personalization options.

Tips: Disabling personalization limits how TikTok tracks you across apps and websites.

Clear TikTok Watch and Search History

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Content & display.
  2. Tap Watch history → Clear history.
  3. Go back, tap Search history → Clear all search history.
On Desktop
  1. In Settings, open Privacy → Data (or Watch history, if available).
  2. Clear watch and search history entries.

Tips: Clearing history reduces data used to shape your recommendations and ads.

Download Your TikTok Data

On Mobile
  1. Profile → Menu → Settings and privacy → Account.
  2. Tap Download your data.
  3. Select TikTok Data format (TXT or JSON), choose a date range, and tap Request data.
On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy → Download your data.
  2. Request the archive, then monitor the Download data tab for availability.

Tips: Reviewing your export shows what TikTok stores and can guide what you remove or lock down.
