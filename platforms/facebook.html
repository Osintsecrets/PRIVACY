Facebook

Practical privacy actions you can take today on Facebook.

Navigation Primer

Mobile (Facebook app)

  • App icon: Blue square with white "f". Open it and sign in.
  • Menu (=): Three horizontal lines. Usually bottom-right on iPhone and top-right on many Android. If you don't see =, tap your profile picture (small circle) at top-right, then tap Settings & privacy.
  • Settings & privacy then Settings: Scroll near the bottom of the Menu screen.
  • Accounts Center: Lives inside Settings. It manages Password & security and some privacy controls.

Desktop (computer browser)

  • Go to facebook.com and sign in.
  • Profile picture (top-right): Small circular photo; click it to open the main menu.
  • Settings & privacy then Settings: Found in that dropdown. On some layouts you'll see Settings first, then Accounts Center in the left sidebar.
  • Left sidebar: Navigation list on the left of the Settings page (e.g., Privacy, Your Facebook information, etc.).

If your layout looks different, use the Settings search bar (top of the Settings page) and type the feature name (e.g., "Two-factor", "Public posts").

Change Your Password

On Desktop
  1. Open a browser and go to facebook.com then sign in.
  2. Click your profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  3. In the left sidebar, click Accounts Center then Password and security then Change password.
  4. Enter your current password, then your new password twice then click Save.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app then sign in.
  2. Tap the Menu (=) (iPhone: bottom-right; Android: top-right). If not visible, tap your profile picture (top-right).
  3. Scroll down then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  4. Tap Password and security then Change password then follow the prompts then Save.

Tips: Use at least 12 characters with letters, numbers, and symbols. Don't reuse passwords.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then sign in then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication.
  3. Choose Authenticator app (recommended) or Security key then follow on-screen steps then Turn on.
On Mobile
  1. Open app then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then Two-factor authentication then pick Authenticator app or Security key then finish setup.

Tips: Write down/secure your backup codes. Avoid SMS 2FA if possible.

Add a Passkey (if available)

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then Passkeys then follow device prompts (Face/Touch ID, PIN) then Enable.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then Passkeys then follow prompts then Enable.

Tips: Passkeys are phishing-resistant and tie login to your device.

Review & Log Out Unknown Sessions

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then Where you're logged in.
  3. Review the list of devices and locations then click ... next to anything unfamiliar then Log out.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then Where you're logged in then tap any unknown session then Log out.

Tips: Do this monthly, especially after travel or using shared devices.

Turn On Login Alerts

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then find Alerts/Notifications about unrecognized logins then Turn on for email and notifications.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then enable alerts for unrecognized logins.

Tips: Choose both Email and Push notification for redundancy.

Update Recovery Contact Info

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Personal details then Contact info then Add new email/phone then verify then ensure visibility of these fields is Only me (adjust under Privacy for profile fields).
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center then Personal details then Contact info.
  2. Add/verify email or phone then set to Only me in your profile's audience controls.

Tips: Use a private recovery email that isn't publicly visible on your profile.

Add a Hardware Security Key

On Desktop
  1. Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication.
  2. Choose Add security key then insert or tap your key when prompted then name it then Save.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings then Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication then Add security key then follow prompts.

Tips: Keep a spare key stored safely in case you lose one.

Remove Remembered Devices (2FA)

On Desktop
  1. Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication then Remembered devices.
  2. Click Remove next to any device you don't control.
On Mobile
  1. Same path then remove remembered devices.

Tips: Always remove devices after using a public/shared computer.

Clear Saved Logins on Shared Devices

On Desktop
  1. On the Facebook login screen, uncheck Keep me signed in.
  2. In your browser's Password/Autofill settings, delete any saved Facebook entries.
On Mobile
  1. Open your device Password manager (iOS Keychain / Android Google Password Manager).
  2. Search for facebook.com then delete saved credentials.

Tips: Avoid saving credentials on borrowed or shared devices.

Recognize & Avoid Phishing

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings & privacy then Settings then Security and login to check official alerts.
  2. Ignore emails/SMS asking for passwords or codes; instead, go directly to facebook.com and check Security and login.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Security and login for official alerts.
  2. Never send codes/links in Messenger/DMs.

Tips: Meta will not ask for your password via email/text.

Set Default Audience for Future Posts

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Left sidebar: Privacy or Audience and visibility then Posts.
  3. Who can see your future posts? then choose Friends or Only me.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Audience and visibility then Posts.
  2. Tap Who can see your future posts? then select Friends/Only me.

Tips: This sets the default for new posts (you can still change per-post).

Limit Past Post Audience (Bulk)

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Audience and visibility then Posts.
  2. Click Limit who can see past posts then read the warning then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Same path then Limit who can see past posts then Confirm.

Tips: Changes old Public/Friends-of-friends posts to Friends.

Hide Your Friends List

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Audience and visibility then How people find and contact you.
  2. Who can see your Friends list? then choose Only me (recommended) or Friends.
On Mobile
  1. Same then set to Only me.

Tips: Hides your social graph from strangers and scrapers.

Restrict Friend Requests

On Desktop
  1. Settings then How people find and contact you.
  2. Who can send you friend requests? then Friends of friends.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Reduces spam/fake requests.

Restrict Lookup by Email

On Desktop
  1. Settings then How people find and contact you.
  2. Who can look you up using the email address you provided? then Only me.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Prevents data-broker matching.

Restrict Lookup by Phone Number

On Desktop
  1. Same page as #15 then set to Only me.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: If you use your real number, this is essential.

Disable Search-Engine Linking to Your Profile

On Desktop
  1. Settings then How people find and contact you.
  2. Toggle off Do you want search engines outside of Facebook to link to your profile?
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Removes your profile from Google/Bing search results.

Hide Active Status (Facebook & Messenger)

On Desktop
  1. Click the Messenger icon (top-right speech bubble) then ... then Active Status.
  2. Toggle Show when you're active then Off.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Active Status.
  2. Toggle Show when you're active then Off.

Tips: Others won't see when you're online.

Turn On Timeline Review (Approve Posts You're Tagged In)

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Profile and tagging.
  2. Enable Review posts you're tagged in before the post appears on your profile.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Prevents surprise content appearing on your timeline.

Turn On Tag Review (Approve Tags People Add)

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Profile and tagging.
  2. Enable Review tags people add to your posts before the tags appear.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Gives you veto power over tags.

Restrict Who Can Post on Your Profile

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Profile and tagging.
  2. Who can post on your profile? then Only you.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Stops others from posting directly to your timeline.

Limit Comments on Your Public Posts

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Audience and visibility then Public posts.
  2. Who can comment on your public posts? then Friends or Profiles you mention.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Reduces spam and harassment on public content.

Delete a Post

On Desktop
  1. Go to facebook.com then click your name to open your Profile.
  2. Scroll to the post then click ... (top-right of the post) then Move to trash or Delete then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. App then tap your profile picture or name to open Profile.
  2. Find the post then tap ... (top-right of the post card) then Delete then Confirm.

Tips: Items in Trash auto-delete after ~30 days unless you empty Trash sooner.

Delete a Photo

On Desktop
  1. Profile then Photos (tabs under your cover area) then Your photos.
  2. Click a photo to open it then click ... (top-right of the photo) then Delete photo then Delete.
On Mobile
  1. Profile then scroll to Photos then Uploads.
  2. Tap a photo then ... (top-right) then Delete photo then Delete.

Tips: Deleting removes the photo, comments, reactions, and tags tied to it.

Remove Your Tag from a Photo or Post

On Desktop
  1. Open the tagged post/photo (it will say "You're tagged").
  2. Click ... (top-right of the post/photo) then Remove tag then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Open the tagged content then tap ... then Remove tag then Confirm.

Tips: This stops the item from appearing on your profile; it does not delete the original.

Delete a Comment You Made

On Desktop
  1. Go to the post with your comment.
  2. Hover over your comment then click ... (to the right) then Delete then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Open the post then find your comment then press and hold the comment.
  2. Tap Delete then Confirm.

Tips: Deleting is permanent; you can also Edit instead.

Remove a Reaction or Unlike a Post

On Desktop
  1. Open the post then click your reaction icon again (it toggles off), or
  2. Profile then Settings & privacy then Activity log then Interactions then Likes and reactions then remove entries.
On Mobile
  1. Open the post then tap your reaction again to remove.
  2. Or Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Activity log then remove reactions.

Tips: Clean up old reactions to reduce your visible activity trail.

Remove Check-Ins and Location Tags

On Desktop
  1. Profile then Settings & privacy then Activity log.
  2. In the left filters, open Logged actions/Location history then delete check-ins or edit posts to remove locations.
On Mobile
  1. Open the post with the location tag then ... then Edit post then remove the location (pin icon) then Save.

Tips: Also review your device's location permissions for Facebook (see later guides).

Archive or Delete Old Stories

On Desktop
  1. Profile then click your profile picture then Story Archive.
  2. Open a story then ... (top-right) then Delete (or leave it archived).
On Mobile
  1. App home then tap your profile picture (or Your story) then Archive.
  2. Open a story then ... then Delete.

Tips: Stories vanish after 24h but live on in Archive until you delete them.

Review and Lock Down Albums

On Desktop
  1. Profile then Photos then Albums.
  2. Hover an album then click ... or Edit then set Audience to Friends/Only me then Save.
On Mobile
  1. Profile then Photos then Albums then open an album then ... then Edit then Audience then choose Friends/Only me then Save.

Tips: Pay special attention to old albums and default albums (e.g., Profile Pictures, Cover Photos).

Review All Tagged Posts and Remove Yourself

On Desktop
  1. Go to facebook.com and log in.
  2. Click your profile picture (top-right) then choose Settings & privacy followed by Settings.
  3. In the left sidebar, click Activity log.
  4. Under Activity you're tagged in, select Posts and comments.
  5. Review each post, click ..., choose Remove tag, then confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Menu (≡), then tap Settings & privacy followed by Settings.
  3. Scroll down and tap Activity log.
  4. Tap Activity you're tagged in, then choose Posts and comments.
  5. Open each post, tap ..., and select Remove tag.

Tips: Removing a tag stops the post from appearing on your profile but doesn't delete it from Facebook.

Disable Face Recognition (if available)

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings & privacy then click Settings.
  2. In the left sidebar, select Face recognition.
  3. Set Do you want Facebook to recognize you in photos and videos? to No.
On Mobile
  1. In the Facebook app, tap the Menu (≡) then choose Settings & privacy followed by Settings.
  2. Scroll to Face recognition.
  3. Select No.

Tips: This prevents Facebook from using facial recognition to tag you automatically.

Restrict Who Can See Your Future Stories

On Desktop
  1. From facebook.com, open Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Choose Audience and visibility then click Stories.
  3. Under Who can see your stories, pick Friends or Only me.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app, tap the Menu (≡), then select Settings & privacy followed by Settings.
  2. Under Audience and visibility, tap Stories.
  3. Choose Who can see your stories? then select Friends or Only me.

Tips: Avoid Public for stories to prevent strangers from viewing them.

Hide Stories from Specific People

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings then go to Audience and visibility followed by Stories.
  2. Under Hide story from, type the names of the people you want to exclude.
On Mobile
  1. Launch the Facebook app and sign in.
  2. Tap the Menu (≡), then tap Settings & privacy and Settings.
  3. Under Audience and visibility, choose Stories.
  4. Tap Hide story from then select the friends you want to exclude.

Tips: Useful if you want certain friends not to see your stories.

Control Who Can Reply to Your Stories

On Desktop
  1. In Settings, open Audience and visibility then Stories.
  2. Under Allow replies and reactions, choose Friends or Off.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app and go to Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Select Audience and visibility → Stories.
  3. Tap Allow replies and reactions and set it to Friends or Off.

Tips: Turning off replies reduces unwanted messages.

Restrict Story Sharing

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Audience and visibility → Stories.
  2. Set Allow others to share your stories to their own story to Off.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app, then navigate to Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Tap Stories under Audience and visibility.
  3. Turn Allow others to share your stories to their own story to Off.

Tips: Stops others from sharing your stories beyond your intended audience.

Turn Off Story Archive

On Desktop
  1. Visit your Profile then click Story Archive.
  2. Click the Settings (gear) icon and toggle Archive stories to Off.
On Mobile
  1. From your Profile, tap Story Archive.
  2. Tap the gear icon, then toggle Archive stories to Off.

Tips: Prevents Facebook from storing past stories.

Restrict Reels Audience

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings then go to Audience and visibility → Reels.
  2. Select Friends or Only me under the audience options.
On Mobile
  1. In the Facebook app, tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Tap Reels under Audience and visibility.
  3. Choose Friends or Only me.

Tips: Reels may default to public unless you change this.

Disable Remixing of Your Reels

On Desktop
  1. Within Settings, open Reels.
  2. Set Allow remixing to Off.
On Mobile
  1. Use the Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings path.
  2. Tap Reels then change Allow remixing to Off.

Tips: Prevents others from reusing your Reels in their videos.

Restrict Comments on Your Reels

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Reels.
  2. Under Who can comment, choose Friends or Off.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Open Reels, then set Who can comment to Friends or Off.

Tips: Stops strangers from commenting on your Reels.

Disable Suggested Reels Featuring Your Content

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings and select Reels.
  2. Turn Suggest my reels on others' feeds to Off.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Reels.
  2. Set Suggest my reels on others' feeds to Off.

Tips: Keeps your content within your chosen audience.

Restrict Who Can Follow You

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings then click Public posts.
  2. Set Who can follow me to Friends.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app, tap Menu (≡), then choose Settings & privacy → Settings.
  2. Scroll to Public posts and set Who can follow me to Friends.

Tips: Prevents strangers from following your public updates.

Block Unwanted Accounts

On Desktop
  1. In Settings, open Blocking.
  2. Enter the person's name or profile link and click Block.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings → Blocking.
  2. Tap Add to blocked list then search for the person and confirm.

Tips: Blocking removes them from your friends list and prevents all contact.

Manage Blocked Apps and Websites

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Apps and websites.
  2. Remove or block any apps you no longer trust.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings → Apps and websites.
  2. Remove or block apps you no longer trust.

Tips: Third-party apps can access your profile data.

Remove App Permissions

On Desktop
  1. In Settings, open Apps and websites then Logged in with Facebook.
  2. Click Remove next to any unwanted app.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings → Apps and websites.
  2. Under Logged in with Facebook, tap an app then choose Remove.

Tips: Regularly audit apps linked to your account.

Turn Off Off-Facebook Activity Tracking

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Your Facebook information → Off-Facebook activity.
  2. Click Clear history, then select Manage future activity and turn it Off.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Your Facebook information.
  2. Choose Off-Facebook activity, tap Clear history, then set Manage future activity to Off.

Tips: This stops Facebook from tracking activity on other sites.

Limit Ad Topics

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings → Ads → Ad topics.
  2. Select each sensitive topic and choose Show less.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Ads.
  2. Tap Ad topics, select a topic, then choose Show less.

Tips: Reduces profiling and targeted ads.

Hide Ads from Specific Advertisers

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Ads → Advertisers.
  2. Click Hide ads from this advertiser for any you don't want to see.
On Mobile
  1. Open Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Ads.
  2. Tap Advertisers, then choose Hide ads from this advertiser.

Tips: Stop seeing content from unwanted advertisers.

Disable Personalized Ads Based on Partners

On Desktop
  1. Within Settings, open Ads → Ad settings.
  2. Set Data about your activity from partners to Not allowed.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Ads.
  2. Open Ad settings and set Data about your activity from partners to Not allowed.

Tips: Prevents ads based on your off-Facebook browsing.

Disable Ads Based on Your Activity on Facebook

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings → Ads → Ad settings.
  2. Set Data about your activity on Facebook to Not allowed.
On Mobile
  1. Navigate to Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Ads.
  2. In Ad settings, change Data about your activity on Facebook to Not allowed.

Tips: Prevents behavior-based targeting.

Disable Ads Based on Your Friends' Activity

On Desktop
  1. Within Ad settings, open Ads shown off Facebook.
  2. Set the audience to No one.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Ads.
  2. Open Ad settings, then set Ads shown off Facebook to No one.

Tips: Stops advertisers from using your interactions to promote ads.

Review and Clear Search History

On Desktop
  1. Click the Search bar at the top of Facebook.
  2. Choose Edit, then click Clear searches.
On Mobile
  1. Tap the Search icon in the app.
  2. Tap Edit, then choose Clear all searches.

Tips: Prevents your search activity from informing recommendations.

Manage Location History

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Location → Location history.
  2. Turn location history off and delete existing history.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Location.
  2. Open Location history, turn it Off, then tap Delete history.

Tips: Disabling this prevents Facebook from tracking your movements.

Disable Precise Location Access

On Mobile
  1. Open your phone's Settings → Apps → Facebook → Permissions.
  2. Select Location and set it to Deny or While using the app.

Tips: Prevents Facebook from accessing your GPS data.

Turn Off Nearby Friends

On Desktop
  1. Navigate to Settings → Location → Nearby friends.
  2. Switch Nearby friends to Off.
On Mobile
  1. Tap Menu (≡) → Settings → Location → Nearby friends.
  2. Toggle Nearby friends to Off.

Tips: Stops Facebook from sharing your location with friends.

Turn Off Location for Stories

On Mobile
  1. When creating a story, tap the Location sticker and choose Remove.
  2. Alternatively, go to Settings → Stories → Location sharing and turn it Off.

Tips: Prevents revealing your location when posting stories.

Turn Off Location for Posts

On Desktop
  1. In the post composer, click the Location pin.
  2. Select Remove to strip the location.
On Mobile
  1. In the post composer, tap Location.
  2. Choose Remove to take location off the post.

Tips: Keeps posts location-agnostic.

Restrict Who Can See Your Check-Ins

On Desktop
  1. Open any of your Check-in posts and click ....
  2. Select Edit audience then choose Only me or Friends.
On Mobile
  1. Tap the Check-in post then tap ....
  2. Select Edit audience and pick Only me or Friends.

Tips: Reduces risks from real-time location disclosure.

Remove Your Location from Photo Metadata

On Desktop
  1. Before uploading, remove EXIF location data from your photos using photo editing software or camera settings.
  2. Upload only the cleaned images to Facebook.
On Mobile
  1. Open your phone's Photos app and select the picture.
  2. Tap Edit info (or similar) and remove the location data before uploading.

Tips: Facebook can read embedded metadata.

Download a Copy of Your Facebook Data

On Desktop
  1. Go to Settings → Your Facebook information → Download your information.
  2. Choose the date range, data types, and format, then click Create file.
On Mobile
  1. Open Menu (≡) → Settings & privacy → Settings → Your Facebook information.
  2. Tap Download your information, adjust the filters, then tap Create file.

Tips: Useful for auditing what data Facebook holds about you.
