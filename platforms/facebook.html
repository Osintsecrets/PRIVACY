Facebook

Practical privacy actions you can take today on Facebook.

Navigation Primer

Mobile (Facebook app)

  • App icon: Blue square with white "f". Open it and sign in.
  • Menu (=): Three horizontal lines. Usually bottom-right on iPhone and top-right on many Android. If you don't see =, tap your profile picture (small circle) at top-right, then tap Settings & privacy.
  • Settings & privacy then Settings: Scroll near the bottom of the Menu screen.
  • Accounts Center: Lives inside Settings. It manages Password & security and some privacy controls.

Desktop (computer browser)

  • Go to facebook.com and sign in.
  • Profile picture (top-right): Small circular photo; click it to open the main menu.
  • Settings & privacy then Settings: Found in that dropdown. On some layouts you'll see Settings first, then Accounts Center in the left sidebar.
  • Left sidebar: Navigation list on the left of the Settings page (e.g., Privacy, Your Facebook information, etc.).

If your layout looks different, use the Settings search bar (top of the Settings page) and type the feature name (e.g., "Two-factor", "Public posts").

Change Your Password

On Desktop
  1. Open a browser and go to facebook.com then sign in.
  2. Click your profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  3. In the left sidebar, click Accounts Center then Password and security then Change password.
  4. Enter your current password, then your new password twice then click Save.
On Mobile
  1. Open the Facebook app then sign in.
  2. Tap the Menu (=) (iPhone: bottom-right; Android: top-right). If not visible, tap your profile picture (top-right).
  3. Scroll down then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  4. Tap Password and security then Change password then follow the prompts then Save.

Tips: Use at least 12 characters with letters, numbers, and symbols. Don't reuse passwords.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then sign in then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication.
  3. Choose Authenticator app (recommended) or Security key then follow on-screen steps then Turn on.
On Mobile
  1. Open app then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then Two-factor authentication then pick Authenticator app or Security key then finish setup.

Tips: Write down/secure your backup codes. Avoid SMS 2FA if possible.

Add a Passkey (if available)

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then Passkeys then follow device prompts (Face/Touch ID, PIN) then Enable.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then Passkeys then follow prompts then Enable.

Tips: Passkeys are phishing-resistant and tie login to your device.

Review & Log Out Unknown Sessions

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then Where you're logged in.
  3. Review the list of devices and locations then click ... next to anything unfamiliar then Log out.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then Where you're logged in then tap any unknown session then Log out.

Tips: Do this monthly, especially after travel or using shared devices.

Turn On Login Alerts

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Password and security then find Alerts/Notifications about unrecognized logins then Turn on for email and notifications.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center.
  2. Password and security then enable alerts for unrecognized logins.

Tips: Choose both Email and Push notification for redundancy.

Update Recovery Contact Info

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Accounts Center then Personal details then Contact info then Add new email/phone then verify then ensure visibility of these fields is Only me (adjust under Privacy for profile fields).
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center then Personal details then Contact info.
  2. Add/verify email or phone then set to Only me in your profile's audience controls.

Tips: Use a private recovery email that isn't publicly visible on your profile.

Add a Hardware Security Key

On Desktop
  1. Settings & privacy then Settings then Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication.
  2. Choose Add security key then insert or tap your key when prompted then name it then Save.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings then Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication then Add security key then follow prompts.

Tips: Keep a spare key stored safely in case you lose one.

Remove Remembered Devices (2FA)

On Desktop
  1. Accounts Center then Password and security then Two-factor authentication then Remembered devices.
  2. Click Remove next to any device you don't control.
On Mobile
  1. Same path then remove remembered devices.

Tips: Always remove devices after using a public/shared computer.

Clear Saved Logins on Shared Devices

On Desktop
  1. On the Facebook login screen, uncheck Keep me signed in.
  2. In your browser's Password/Autofill settings, delete any saved Facebook entries.
On Mobile
  1. Open your device Password manager (iOS Keychain / Android Google Password Manager).
  2. Search for facebook.com then delete saved credentials.

Tips: Avoid saving credentials on borrowed or shared devices.

Recognize & Avoid Phishing

On Desktop
  1. Open Settings & privacy then Settings then Security and login to check official alerts.
  2. Ignore emails/SMS asking for passwords or codes; instead, go directly to facebook.com and check Security and login.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Security and login for official alerts.
  2. Never send codes/links in Messenger/DMs.

Tips: Meta will not ask for your password via email/text.

Set Default Audience for Future Posts

On Desktop
  1. facebook.com then profile picture (top-right) then Settings & privacy then Settings.
  2. Left sidebar: Privacy or Audience and visibility then Posts.
  3. Who can see your future posts? then choose Friends or Only me.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Audience and visibility then Posts.
  2. Tap Who can see your future posts? then select Friends/Only me.

Tips: This sets the default for new posts (you can still change per-post).

Limit Past Post Audience (Bulk)

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Audience and visibility then Posts.
  2. Click Limit who can see past posts then read the warning then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Same path then Limit who can see past posts then Confirm.

Tips: Changes old Public/Friends-of-friends posts to Friends.

Hide Your Friends List

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Audience and visibility then How people find and contact you.
  2. Who can see your Friends list? then choose Only me (recommended) or Friends.
On Mobile
  1. Same then set to Only me.

Tips: Hides your social graph from strangers and scrapers.

Restrict Friend Requests

On Desktop
  1. Settings then How people find and contact you.
  2. Who can send you friend requests? then Friends of friends.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Reduces spam/fake requests.

Restrict Lookup by Email

On Desktop
  1. Settings then How people find and contact you.
  2. Who can look you up using the email address you provided? then Only me.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Prevents data-broker matching.

Restrict Lookup by Phone Number

On Desktop
  1. Same page as #15 then set to Only me.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: If you use your real number, this is essential.

Disable Search-Engine Linking to Your Profile

On Desktop
  1. Settings then How people find and contact you.
  2. Toggle off Do you want search engines outside of Facebook to link to your profile?
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Removes your profile from Google/Bing search results.

Hide Active Status (Facebook & Messenger)

On Desktop
  1. Click the Messenger icon (top-right speech bubble) then ... then Active Status.
  2. Toggle Show when you're active then Off.
On Mobile
  1. App then Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Settings then Active Status.
  2. Toggle Show when you're active then Off.

Tips: Others won't see when you're online.

Turn On Timeline Review (Approve Posts You're Tagged In)

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Profile and tagging.
  2. Enable Review posts you're tagged in before the post appears on your profile.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Prevents surprise content appearing on your timeline.

Turn On Tag Review (Approve Tags People Add)

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Profile and tagging.
  2. Enable Review tags people add to your posts before the tags appear.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Gives you veto power over tags.

Restrict Who Can Post on Your Profile

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Profile and tagging.
  2. Who can post on your profile? then Only you.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Stops others from posting directly to your timeline.

Limit Comments on Your Public Posts

On Desktop
  1. Settings then Audience and visibility then Public posts.
  2. Who can comment on your public posts? then Friends or Profiles you mention.
On Mobile
  1. Same.

Tips: Reduces spam and harassment on public content.

Delete a Post

On Desktop
  1. Go to facebook.com then click your name to open your Profile.
  2. Scroll to the post then click ... (top-right of the post) then Move to trash or Delete then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. App then tap your profile picture or name to open Profile.
  2. Find the post then tap ... (top-right of the post card) then Delete then Confirm.

Tips: Items in Trash auto-delete after ~30 days unless you empty Trash sooner.

Delete a Photo

On Desktop
  1. Profile then Photos (tabs under your cover area) then Your photos.
  2. Click a photo to open it then click ... (top-right of the photo) then Delete photo then Delete.
On Mobile
  1. Profile then scroll to Photos then Uploads.
  2. Tap a photo then ... (top-right) then Delete photo then Delete.

Tips: Deleting removes the photo, comments, reactions, and tags tied to it.

Remove Your Tag from a Photo or Post

On Desktop
  1. Open the tagged post/photo (it will say "You're tagged").
  2. Click ... (top-right of the post/photo) then Remove tag then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Open the tagged content then tap ... then Remove tag then Confirm.

Tips: This stops the item from appearing on your profile; it does not delete the original.

Delete a Comment You Made

On Desktop
  1. Go to the post with your comment.
  2. Hover over your comment then click ... (to the right) then Delete then Confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Open the post then find your comment then press and hold the comment.
  2. Tap Delete then Confirm.

Tips: Deleting is permanent; you can also Edit instead.

Remove a Reaction or Unlike a Post

On Desktop
  1. Open the post then click your reaction icon again (it toggles off), or
  2. Profile then Settings & privacy then Activity log then Interactions then Likes and reactions then remove entries.
On Mobile
  1. Open the post then tap your reaction again to remove.
  2. Or Menu (=) then Settings & privacy then Activity log then remove reactions.

Tips: Clean up old reactions to reduce your visible activity trail.

Remove Check-Ins and Location Tags

On Desktop
  1. Profile then Settings & privacy then Activity log.
  2. In the left filters, open Logged actions/Location history then delete check-ins or edit posts to remove locations.
On Mobile
  1. Open the post with the location tag then ... then Edit post then remove the location (pin icon) then Save.

Tips: Also review your device's location permissions for Facebook (see later guides).

Archive or Delete Old Stories

On Desktop
  1. Profile then click your profile picture then Story Archive.
  2. Open a story then ... (top-right) then Delete (or leave it archived).
On Mobile
  1. App home then tap your profile picture (or Your story) then Archive.
  2. Open a story then ... then Delete.

Tips: Stories vanish after 24h but live on in Archive until you delete them.

Review and Lock Down Albums

On Desktop
  1. Profile then Photos then Albums.
  2. Hover an album then click ... or Edit then set Audience to Friends/Only me then Save.
On Mobile
  1. Profile then Photos then Albums then open an album then ... then Edit then Audience then choose Friends/Only me then Save.

Tips: Pay special attention to old albums and default albums (e.g., Profile Pictures, Cover Photos).
