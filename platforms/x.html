Change Your X Password

Path: Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Your account → Change your password

Web (browser)
  1. Go to twitter.com and sign in.
  2. Click More (left sidebar) → Settings and privacy.
  3. Click Your account → Change your password.
  4. Enter your current password, type a strong new password twice, and click Save.
  5. Confirm: Click Save to confirm the new password.
Android app
  1. Open the X app.
  2. Tap your profile photo (top left) → Settings and privacy.
  3. Tap Your account → Change your password.
  4. Enter your current password, type a strong new password twice, and tap Update password.
  5. Confirm: Tap Save to finish.
iOS app
  1. Open the X app on iOS.
  2. Tap your profile photo (top left) → Settings and privacy.
  3. Tap Your account → Change your password.
  4. Enter your current password, type a strong new password twice, and tap Update password.
  5. Confirm: Tap Save to finish.

Reset X App Permissions on Your Phone

Path: Device settings → Apps → X → Permissions

Web (browser)
  1. This action must be completed on your phone.
  2. Open your device settings and search for the X app.
  3. Open Permissions to review camera, microphone, contacts, and storage access.
  4. Disable any permission the app doesn’t need.
  5. Confirm: Save or back out to apply the new permission state.
Android app
  1. Open Android Settings → Apps → X.
  2. Tap Permissions to review camera, microphone, contacts, and storage access.
  3. Turn off any permission the app doesn’t need.
  4. Back out to make sure the change sticks.
  5. Confirm: Tap Don’t allow when asked to confirm.
iOS app
  1. Open iOS Settings → X.
  2. Review toggles for Camera, Microphone, Contacts, Bluetooth, and Photos.
  3. Turn off any permission you no longer want the app to use.
  4. Return to save the new state.
  5. Confirm: Tap Don’t Allow if iOS prompts for confirmation.
