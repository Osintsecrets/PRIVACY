X (Twitter)

Practical privacy actions you can take today on X.

No actions matched your search. Try different keywords or remove filters.

Navigation Primer

Mobile (X app)

  • Tap your profile photo (top-left) to open the main menu.
  • Select Settings and privacy from the menu for account and privacy controls.
  • Inside Settings, use Search settings (top bar) to jump to specific features like “two-factor”.
  • Privacy controls live under Privacy and safety; security controls are under Security and account access.

Desktop (twitter.com)

  • Go to twitter.com and sign in.
  • Click More (left sidebar) → Settings and privacy.
  • Use the left sidebar in Settings to switch between Your account, Privacy and safety, and Security and account access.
  • The search field at the top of Settings helps you locate features quickly.

If the menu changes wording, look for “Settings and privacy” and then the section that best matches the task (Security, Privacy, or Your account).

Guides #1–12

Change Your X Password

On Desktop
  1. Go to twitter.com → More → Settings and privacy.
  2. Select Your account → Change your password.
  3. Enter your current password, then a strong new password twice, and click Save.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Your account.
  2. Tap Change your password.
  3. Enter your current password and create a new one, then tap Update password.

Tips: Use a unique password that isn’t shared with any other site.

Enable X Two-Factor Authentication

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Security and account access → Security → Two-factor authentication.
  2. Enable Authentication app or Security key (avoid SMS when possible).
  3. Follow the prompts to scan the QR code or register your key, then confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Security and account access → Security.
  2. Tap Two-factor authentication and enable Authentication app or Security key.
  3. Complete the setup steps and confirm.

Tips: Pair an authenticator app with backup codes to avoid lockouts.

Generate X Backup Codes

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Security and account access → Security → Two-factor authentication.
  2. Select Backup codes → Generate backup codes.
  3. Download or print the codes and store them securely offline.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Security and account access → Security.
  2. Tap Two-factor authentication → Backup codes → Generate.
  3. Copy the codes to a safe password manager or encrypted note.

Tips: Backup codes let you sign in if you lose your phone or hardware key.

Review X Active Sessions

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Security and account access → Apps and sessions.
  2. Click Sessions.
  3. Select Log out of all other sessions, then individually log out of anything unfamiliar.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Security and account access → Apps and sessions.
  2. Tap Sessions.
  3. Log out of devices you do not recognize.

Tips: Clearing old sessions stops former devices from regaining access.

Update X Account Information

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Your account → Account information (enter your password if prompted).
  2. Update your email and phone number, verify them, and ensure they’re private.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Your account → Account information.
  2. Review and update contact details, then confirm verification codes.

Tips: Use a secure email address that you check regularly and keep phone numbers up to date.

Protect Your Posts (Private Account)

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Audience and tagging.
  2. Toggle Protect your posts on, then confirm.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Privacy and safety → Audience and tagging.
  2. Enable Protect your posts.

Tips: When protected, only approved followers see your posts and past content.

Control X Direct Messages

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Direct messages.
  2. Disable Allow message requests from everyone.
  3. Turn off Allow requests from verified users unless needed.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Privacy and safety → Direct messages.
  2. Adjust toggles to limit who can DM you and whether read receipts are sent.

Tips: Restricting DM requests blocks spam and phishing attempts.

Disable Discoverability by Email or Phone

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Discoverability and contacts.
  2. Uncheck Let people who have your email address find you on X and the phone option too.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Privacy and safety → Discoverability and contacts.
  2. Turn off both discoverability toggles.

Tips: Disabling discoverability keeps your account hidden from people uploading contact lists.

Limit X Ad Personalization

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Ads preferences.
  2. Toggle off Personalized ads and Inferred interests.
  3. Review the interest list and uncheck anything sensitive.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Privacy and safety → Ads preferences.
  2. Disable personalized ads and clear stored interests.

Tips: Turning off personalization reduces tracking and profiling for advertising.

Turn Off Location Tagging on X

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Privacy and safety → Location information.
  2. Disable Add location information to your Posts.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Privacy and safety → Location information.
  2. Turn off adding location to posts and clear past location data if available.

Tips: Without location tags, posts reveal less about your real-world movements.

Remove Connected Apps on X

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Security and account access → Apps and sessions → Connected apps.
  2. Click any app you no longer use → Revoke app permissions.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Security and account access → Apps and sessions.
  2. Tap Connected apps and revoke access for untrusted apps.

Tips: Removing unused apps stops them from reading your DMs or posting on your behalf.

Download Your X Data Archive

On Desktop
  1. Settings → Your account → Download an archive of your data.
  2. Verify your password and email/phone, then request the archive.
  3. Check your email or notifications when the archive is ready to download.
On Mobile
  1. Profile photo → Settings and privacy → Your account → Download an archive of your data.
  2. Authenticate, request the file, and wait for the notification to download.

Tips: Reviewing your archive shows what data X stores and highlights old content to remove.
