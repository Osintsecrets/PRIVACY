WhatsApp — Privacy & Security

Enable Two-Step Verification PIN

Path: Settings → Account → Two-step verification

Web (browser)
  1. (Not available on Web)
Android app
  1. Open WhatsApp → tap the ⋮ menu → Settings → Account.
  2. Tap Two-step verification → Turn on.
  3. Create a 6-digit PIN, confirm it, then add an email address for recovery.
  4. Tap Save to finish.
iOS app
  1. Open WhatsApp → tap Settings → Account.
  2. Choose Two-step verification → Turn on.
  3. Enter and confirm a 6-digit PIN, then add an email address.
  4. Tap Save to enable it.

Update Two-Step Verification PIN

Path: Settings → Account → Two-step verification

Web (browser)
  1. (Not available on Web)
Android app
  1. WhatsApp → ⋮ → Settings → Account.
  2. Tap Two-step verification → Change PIN.
  3. Enter your current PIN, then set and confirm a new PIN.
  4. Tap Save.
iOS app
  1. WhatsApp → Settings → Account.
  2. Select Two-step verification → Change PIN.
  3. Enter the existing PIN, then enter and confirm the new PIN.
  4. Tap Save.

Reset Two-Step Verification PIN via Email

Path: Settings → Account → Two-step verification

Web (browser)
  1. (Not available on Web)
Android app
  1. Open WhatsApp → enter your email on the PIN prompt → tap Forgot PIN?.
  2. Tap Send email to receive the reset link.
  3. Open the email on your phone and tap the reset link.
  4. Set and confirm a new PIN, then tap Save.
iOS app
  1. Open WhatsApp → tap Forgot PIN? on the verification screen.
  2. Choose Send email to receive a reset link.
  3. Open the email and follow the link in Safari.
  4. Create and confirm a new PIN, then tap Save.

Verify a Contact's Security Code

Path: Chat → Contact info → Encryption

Web (browser)
  1. Open web.whatsapp.com and select the chat.
  2. Click the contact or group name to open info.
  3. Click Encryption.
  4. Scan the QR code with your phone or compare the 60-digit number with your contact.
Android app
  1. Open the chat.
  2. Tap the contact or group name to open info.
  3. Scroll to Encryption and tap it.
  4. Scan the QR code or compare the digits.
  5. Tap Verify if prompted.
iOS app
  1. Open the chat.
  2. Tap the contact or group name to open info.
  3. Tap Encryption.
  4. Scan the QR code or compare the digits.
  5. Tap Verify if prompted.

Hide Last Seen and Online Status

Path: Settings → Privacy → Last seen and Online

Web (browser)
  1. Open web.whatsapp.com and link your phone.
  2. Click the ⋮ menu → Settings → Privacy.
  3. Click Last seen and online.
  4. Choose Nobody for Last seen.
  5. Select Same as Last seen for Online status.
  6. Click Done to save.
Android app
  1. Open WhatsApp → tap the ⋮ menu → Settings.
  2. Tap Privacy.
  3. Tap Last seen and online.
  4. Select Nobody under Last seen.
  5. Choose Same as Last seen for Who can see when you're online.
  6. Tap Done.
iOS app
  1. Open WhatsApp → tap Settings.
  2. Tap Privacy.
  3. Tap Last Seen & Online.
  4. Tap Nobody under Last Seen.
  5. Choose Same as Last Seen for Online status.
  6. Tap Done.
