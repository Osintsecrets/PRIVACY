Install on your device

Install prompts appear when your browser supports them.

Using Safari on iPhone or iPad

  1. Tap the Share button in Safari’s toolbar.
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen from the sheet.
  3. Review the name and tap Add to finish.

This adds the site to your Home Screen with its own icon and fullscreen experience.
