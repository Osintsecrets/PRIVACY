Ethical Ground Rules

Protecting privacy is a responsibility. These principles keep the work focused on care, not harm.

Principles we follow

  • Do no harm: every action should reduce risk, not create it.
  • Gain consent whenever possible and respect when people say no.
  • Stay within the law and platform rules.
  • Respect bystanders and avoid exposing information that is not yours.
  • Prioritize accuracy and verify before sharing sensitive findings.

What this site is not

This is not a toolkit for harassment, doxxing, or digging into private lives without permission. The walkthroughs here are designed to empower individuals to control their own data and reduce the risk of abuse.
