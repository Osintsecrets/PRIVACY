Contact the Social Risk Audit team

We welcome responsible disclosures, collaboration requests, and questions about privacy safeguards. Use the channel that best matches the urgency of your message and include only the information necessary for us to respond.

General inquiries

For most questions, community partnerships, or OSINT hygiene tips, email us at contact@osintsecrets.org.

Including the details below helps us reply quickly:

  • Summary of your request or question (2–3 sentences).
  • Relevant links, screenshots, or affected services.
  • Your preferred response window and contact method.

You can also reference this address in third-party vendor forms. Our inbox is monitored on business days (UTC).

Urgent safety escalations

If you believe someone is at immediate risk of doxxing, stalking, or coordinated harassment, flag it as URGENT in your subject line and email safety@osintsecrets.org.

Include only essentials:

  • Who is affected and how we can reach them safely.
  • Links or identifiers that demonstrate the threat.
  • Any steps already taken (law enforcement, platform reports, etc.).

We triage urgent mail 24/7. If a life-threatening emergency is in progress, contact local authorities first.

Secure follow-up options

For sensitive disclosures or if you prefer end-to-end encryption, you can reach us on Matrix at @osintsecrets:matrix.org or via Signal at +1-415-555-0119.

When requesting our public key for file transfers, send an empty email to pgp@osintsecrets.org with the subject line “PGP request”. You will receive an automated reply containing our PGP fingerprint and policy for encrypted submissions.

Secure channels are checked twice daily. Please note that we never request passwords, MFA codes, or payment information.
